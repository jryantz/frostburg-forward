About Us!

Western Maryland BizAssist aims to ease the process of planning and opening a business in Western Maryland, with the goal of boosting regional economy. Frostburg Forward, an organization comprised of economic development officials in the region, had the idea to create a website to facilitate this goal. The organization commissioned a group of Computer Science and Information Technology students from Frostburg State University to create the site.
